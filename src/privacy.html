Privacy

Browsing this site sends us nothing. We do not set cookies. The only information we receive is what you put in an email.

When you visit

We do not set cookies or run analytics. Theme and language preferences stay in your browser and are not sent to us.

The homepage video uses YouTube's privacy-enhanced player (youtube-nocookie.com). It does not set cookies unless you press play.

When you email us

If you write to stories@extraspacescam.com or editor@extraspacescam.com, we receive whatever you include: your email address, your name (if you give it), and the contents of your message.

  • We use it to read your note, reply if needed, verify a story, or fix an error on the site
  • We keep messages only as long as we need for those purposes
  • We do not sell or share your email or message with anyone else
  • We do not publish anything from your email without your explicit permission
  • To ask us to delete something you sent, email editor@extraspacescam.com
