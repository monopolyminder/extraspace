How It's Built

Editorial Standards

This is an independent consumer resource, not affiliated with Extra Space Storage or any competitor. These are the rules every page follows.

What We Cite

  • Court filings, dockets, and settlement records
  • Government agency press releases and complaint portals
  • The company's own public statements, SEC filings, and investor materials
  • Peer-reviewed or academic publications describing company practices
  • Consumer complaint databases with public profiles
  • News reporting from established outlets and trade press

The Rules

  • Major factual claims link to a source you can verify yourself; first-person testimony is clearly labeled and stands on its own
  • Allegations are called allegations. Where a case settled, we say so, including when the company admitted nothing
  • Words like "trap" and "scam" are our opinion of what the record shows; the site disclaimer says exactly that
  • When the public record changes, the pages change, and the footer date changes with them
